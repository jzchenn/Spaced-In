# DEVS x SESA Beginner Hackathon 2026
**Theme:** Out of this World

**Spaced In** is a space-themed web application designed to help students and remote workers minimize distractions and reclaim their focus. As users study in an environment designed to simulate the vast emptiness of space, a planet slowly forms to provide a rewarding and visible indicator of their progress.

## The Problem
Students and remote workers often face academic burnout, low energy, and constant digital distractions that fragment their focus. Without a clear visual of their incremental efforts, it becomes incredibly easy to procrastinate and lose motivation.
## The Solution
**Spaced In** provides a minimalistic, space-themed environment designed to eliminate cognitive overload and keep you "spaced in". The app gamifies productivity by slowly forming a unique planet on your screen as you work, rewarding your focus with a growing cosmic collection.

## Setup Instructions
### Prerequesites
* **Python 3.9+**
* **Node.js (v20.19+ or v22.12+)**

### Backend (FastAPI)
1. `cd backend`
2. `python3 -m venv venv`
3. `source venv/bin/activate` 
4. `pip install -r requirements.txt`
5. `uvicorn main:app --reload`

### Frontend (React + Vite)
1. `cd frontend`
2. `npm install`
3. `npm run dev`
